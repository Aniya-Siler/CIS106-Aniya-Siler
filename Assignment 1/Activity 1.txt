How to make Vegan Pancakes
Open Cabinet
Grab a large bowl out of the Cabinet
Close Cabinet
Open Pantry
Get out the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and brown sugar from pantry
Close Pantry
Open Flour container
Open Cabinet
Grab a measuring cup out of cabinet
Close Cabinet
Pour 1 cup of flour
Open Baking Powder container
Open drawer and grab a tablespoon, 1/4 teaspoon, and a half teaspoon
Close drawer
Open salt shaker
Open cinnamon container
Open brown sugar package
pour 1 tablespoon of baking powder into the large bowl
pour 1/4 teaspoon of salt into the large bowl
2 tablespoons of Cinnamon into the large bowl
1/2 a tablespoon of Brown sugar into the large bowl
Open drawer
Grab spatula
Close Drawer
Stir the dry ingredients together with spatula
Close baking powder container, salt, cinnamon, and brown sugar
Open Fridge
Get out the Non-Dairy Milk, Vegan butter from fridge
Close fridge
Open Cabinet
Get out the vanilla extract from cabinet
Close Cabinet
Take off top to non-dairy milk
Take off the top off Vanilla extract
Cut 2 tablespoons of Vegan butter
Take off wrapper off of the vegan butter
Open Drawer and grab a tablespoon, a measuring cup, a teaspoon, and a half teaspoon
Pour 1 cup of the non dairy milk into the large bowl with the dry ingredients
2 tablespoons of vegan butter into the large bowl with the dry ingredients
1.5 teaspoons of vanilla extract into the large bowl with dry ingredients
Grab Spatula
incorporate ingredients together
Open Cabinet
Grab a non stick pan out of cabinet
Close Cabinet
Turn on stove and heat to medium heat
Place non stick pan on the stove top
Add a teaspoon of butter to the pan
Open drawer
Grab a 1/4 cup
Close drawer
Scoop 1/4 cups of batter into the pan for each pancake
Flip the pancake over so that each side is fully cooked
Open cabinet
take out plate
Close cabinet
Remove pancake from pan and place on the plate
Continue the steps until there is not any batter left
Turn off Stove
Close flour container
Close baking powder container
Close salt shaker
Close Brown sugar container
Close Cinnamon container
Close Vanilla Extract container
Put top back on the non-dairy milk
Open pantry
Put the flour, baking powder, salt, brown sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla extract into pantry
Close Pantry
Open Fridge
Put non-dairy milk, and vegan butter back into the fridge
Close Fridge
Put Large bowl, measuring cup, 1/4 cup, 1 teaspoon, 1/2 teaspoon, tablespoon, 1/4 teaspoon and non stick pan into the sink or dishwasher
Enjoy
