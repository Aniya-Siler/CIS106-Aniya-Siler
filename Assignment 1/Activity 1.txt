How to make Vegan Panecakes
Grab a large bowl out of the Cabinet
Get out the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and brown sugar
Pour 1 cup of flour, 1 tablespoon of baking powder 1/4 teaspoon of salt, 2 tablespoons of Cinnamon, and 1/2 a tablespoon of Brown sugar into the large bowl
Stir the dry ingredients together
Get out the Non-Dairy Milk, Vegan butter from fridge
Get out the vanilla extract from cabinet
Pour 1 cup of the non dairy milk, 2 tablespoos of vegan butter and 1.5 teaspoons of vanilla extract into the large bowl with dry ingredients
incorporate ingredients together
Grab a non stick pan and heat it to medium heat
Add a teaspoon of butter to the pan
Scoop 1/4 cups of batter into the pan for each pancake
Flip the pancake over so that each side is fully cooked
Remove pancake from pan
Continue the steps until there is not any batter left
Put away flour, baking powder, salt, brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla extract, non-dairy milk, and butter
Put Large bowl and non stick pan into the sink or dishwasher
Enjoy
